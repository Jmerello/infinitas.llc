About Us

Infinitas LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) based in Keatchie, Louisiana. Founded in 2025, we supply wholesale goods—medical/ surgical, office, janitorial, groceries, and more—to government and private clients nationwide.

Our veteran-led team partners with trusted suppliers (LOSCO, Sysco, McKesson, ) to deliver fast, reliable drop-shipping, meeting your needs with integrity and efficiency.
